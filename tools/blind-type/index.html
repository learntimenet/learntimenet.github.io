Description

Speed Typing is an online tool designed to help you master Touch Typing (or Blind Typing)—a technique that lets you type quickly and accurately without looking at the keyboard. Every glance at the keys or typo slows you down. With our tool, you’ll train to type faster, more efficiently, and with fewer errors.

Unlike other typing apps, Speed Typing keeps practice engaging by offering:

  • Random text snippets to simulate real-world typing scenarios
  • Inspirational quotes to make practice enjoyable and motivational

Track your progress with Words per minute (WPM) and accuracy metrics
